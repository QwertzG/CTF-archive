## My Solution
- In physics, particles that travel at the speed of light are photons.
- Hypothetical particles that travel faster than the speed of light are known as **Tachyons**.
- Search Query: `"Tachyon horse"` or `"horse faster than light"`
The search results in finding **Agnes Tachyon** which is the answer to this problem
